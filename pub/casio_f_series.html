A "W" after the model number designates the watch as Water Resistant. Some models (like the F-87) exist with and without a W, and differ in the look of the crystal.

The dash-number represents the color:

  • -1 denotes black
  • -3 denotes green
  • -5 denotes brown
  • -7 denotes silver
  • -9 denotes gold
